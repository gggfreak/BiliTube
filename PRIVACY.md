# Privacy Policy

Subtitle Masker does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

## What the extension does

- Injects a local subtitle mask into web pages you open
- Stores mask settings locally in the browser
- Responds to your popup actions and keyboard shortcut

## What data is stored

The extension stores only local configuration data, such as:

- mask visibility
- opacity
- per-site mask position
- per-site mask size

This data stays in your browser's extension storage.

## What is not collected

- No account information
- No browsing history upload
- No video content upload
- No subtitle content upload
- No analytics
- No remote server communication
